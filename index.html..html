Features

Everything your clinic needs, automated

MediBot handles the repetitive, time-consuming patient communication tasks — so your staff can focus on care.

Smart Appointment Booking

Patients book, reschedule, or cancel appointments 24/7. Real-time doctor availability with instant confirmation.

Lab Result Delivery

Securely send lab results directly to patients via WhatsApp after identity verification. No phone tag required.

Prescription Refills

Patients request refills, the bot routes to the prescribing doctor for approval, and notifies when ready.

AI Symptom Triage

Intelligent first assessment guides patients to the right level of care — emergency, urgent, or routine.

Automated Reminders

24-hour and 2-hour appointment reminders reduce no-shows by up to 60%. Patients confirm with one tap.

Post-Visit Follow-up

Automated recovery check-ins improve outcomes and give doctors real-time patient progress updates.

Feedback & Reviews

Collect satisfaction ratings after every visit. Capture referrals while experience is fresh in mind.

Analytics Dashboard

Real-time insights on chat volumes, resolution rates, most common queries, and patient satisfaction scores.

EHR Integration

Connects with existing systems — HealthSuite, PIMS, iClinic, and custom EMR via secure REST APIs.
